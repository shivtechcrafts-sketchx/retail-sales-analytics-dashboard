## 📖 Project Story

A retail company had thousands of sales records but no simple way to understand business performance. Management needed quick answers about regions, categories, customer segments and monthly sales.

This project transforms raw sales data into an interactive Microsoft Excel dashboard using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPIs and Slicers to support business decision-making.

## 🎯 Objectives

- Clean raw data
- Build KPI dashboard
- Perform business analysis
- Create interactive visualizations
- Generate business insights
- Recommend business actions

## 💡 Business Insights

- West region generated the highest sales.
- Technology was the top-performing category.
- Consumer segment contributed the largest share of revenue.
- November recorded peak sales.

## 🚀 Recommendations

- Increase marketing in low-performing regions.
- Invest in Technology category.
- Launch customer loyalty programs.
- Improve inventory planning before peak months.
- Promote complementary products.

## ▶️ How to Use

1. Download the Excel workbook.
2. Open in Microsoft Excel.
3. Use slicers to interact with the dashboard.
